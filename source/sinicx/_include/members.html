• Atsushi Hashimoto

    Atsushi Hashimoto is the Senior Researcher in Perception Group. He has been working on activity-forecast-based human-computer interaction for assembling and cooking tasks.

    He received B.S. degree in Engineering, M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in Informatics from Kyoto University, Japan, in 2005, 2008, and 2013, respectively.

  • Felix von Drigalski

    Felix von Drigalski is the Senior Researcher in Robotics Group. His research interests include robotic manipulation, tactile sensing and automated assembly.

    He earned engineering diplomas (MSc level) from the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology and the INSA Lyon in 2013, and a Ph.D. from the Nara Institute of Science and Technology in 2018 as a MEXT government scholar. His teams took 1st place in the Airbus Shop Floor Challenge at ICRA 2016, 6th place in the Amazon Robotics Challenge 2017 and 4th place in the World Robot Summit 2018 Assembly Challenge.

  • Jiaxin Ma

    Jiaxin Ma is the Senior Researcher in Interaction Group. His research interests include machine learning, human machine interface, and biomedical signal applications.

    He received his Ph.D. degree in Mechanical Engineering and Science from Kyoto Unversity in 2015. In 2012, he was a research intern of RIKEN Brain Science Institute, and a visiting scholar of Johns Hopkins University. During 2015-2018, he was working at Teijin Pharma as a biomedical engineer. He is also a gamer and MTG player.

  • Mitsu Konishi

    Mitsu Konishi is the Director of the Near-future Design Group, where he is responsible for designing the architecture of “autonomous” society based on Omron’s SINIC theory.
