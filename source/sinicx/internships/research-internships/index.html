ADVANCED RESEARCH INTERNSHIPS

We offer short-term internships and research stays to excellent students and researchers both in- and outside of Japan. You will work on a challenging project, independently and in collaboration with our researchers. The topic will be agreed upon during the interview process. Subsequent publication of the results at an international conference or journal is highly encouraged and can be supported after the internship period.

Who is the program for?

  • Students (Master’s and PhD courses) and researchers worldwide

Work environment & location:

  • Business hours: 8 hours/day, 5 days/week (excluding national holidays)
  • Location: Central Tokyo, next to the University of Tokyo
    https://goo.gl/maps/PoD6K8minRJ2
  • Residence within biking distance
  • Dynamic team, flexible working hours, free snacks & coffee

Period & Compensation:

  • The period of the internship can be flexibly arranged
  • We request a minimum of two months (40 business days) in order to be able to tackle a challenging task, and recommend 4-8 months depending on the project
  • Interns are paid a competitive salary (400,000 Yen per month or more)
  • We will cover residence and travel cost

Requirements:

  • Experience in at least one of the technology areas (listed below) other than lectures
    e. g., published a paper, won a competition, part-time work, open source contribution
  • Strong programming skills (C++ or Python)
  • Fluent in either English or Japanese
  • Able to work full-time on weekdays at our Tokyo office

Preferred experience & skills:

  • Machine learning and deep learning
  • Experience with ROS, OpenCV, grasp planning
  • Experience with numpy / scipy / deep learning frameworks
  • Experience with software & service development
  • Experience working with shared codebases (e.g. Github, Gitlab)
  • Contribution to open source projects
  • Experience writing academic papers

Candidate themes (subject to change)

  1. Technology areas: Robotics and machine learning
    1. Deep learning theory
    2. Reinforcement learning
    3. Computer vision
    4. Natural language processing
    5. Sensing / Control theory
  2. Application areas: Advanced applications, such as
    1. Large-scale object detection / memory-efficient high-speed 3D object detection / tracking / segmentation from image / video
    2. Robotic skill learning / object manipulation / simulation
    3. Understanding human action/intention/status from video and EEG
    4. Life science / healthcare
    5. Human machine interaction
    6. Design / content creation / visualization

Application information:

  • Resume / CV (PDF format)
  • Github/Gitlab account (optional)

How to apply:

  • Please submit your CV by email: f.drigalski@sinicx.com
  • The review process takes about 6-8 weeks after submission
  • Getting a visa for working in Japan usually takes up to 3 months

Interview process:

  1. Document review
  2. Aptitude tests (depending on the project)
  3. Skype interview in English or Japanese
