News 15.07.07 OMRON VENTURES to Sponsor Leave a Nest’s “Tech Planter” Seed Acceleration Program

OMRON VENTURES will participate as a sponsor in the “Tech Planter” seed acceleration program organized by Leave a Nest Co., Ltd. (Headquarters: Shinjuku-ku, Tokyo, CEO: Yukihiro Maru). The purpose of the program is to discover and nurture promising startup companies.
