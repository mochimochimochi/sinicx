News 17.06.29 OMRON VENTURES Invests in Lark Technologies, Inc.

OMRON VENTURES Co., Ltd. (Chief Executive Officer: Naoshi Ozawa) announced that it has invested in Lark Technologies, Inc. (Headquarters: California, USA), which develops health-related management applications for improving lifestyle habits.

OMRON VENTURES will support the future growth and development of Lark utilizing OMRON's own technology to jointly create new value in the healthcare field.
